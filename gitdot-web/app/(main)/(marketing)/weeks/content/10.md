Mikkel and I got sick.

We got COVID and didn’t get much done. \
Our hubris still made us try, but as it turns out, vibe-coding on Nyquil is unsurprisingly a bad idea.

Anywho. \
This week, it seems I have little to write about. \
This series has leaned increasingly personal, something I feel uncertain about, but regardless it is serving its purpose well.

It is hard to work by yourself. \
Having someone helps; whether that be your boss, your peers, or your customers. \
So to the you that is reading this now: thanks, it means a lot.

&nbsp;

To end on.

When we left our jobs in April of 2025, the main reason why was fear. \
We thought that it was the last chance to start a business before AI took over.

We believe that less now. \
But regardless of if and when the singularity will come, the lesson it taught is the same.

Our time is limited — and I don't intend to spend a moment of it insincere.

Thank you. \
—baepaul
