date: "Apr 26 - May 03, 2026"

We're getting close.

We've spent the last few months trying a lot of things. \
We embarked with the mission of "building a better GitHub" and in that process, came up with a lot of different definitions of what that could be.

We built a lot. \
Not all of those things were right. \
And even the things we think are right now, will undoubtedly change in the future.

But we're quite certain of what we need to build now. \
More than a vision, we have a plan. And we started to see glimpses of what gitdot could be, of how things could feel once it's all realized.

So it's time to execute. \
The first thing we launch will be incredibly bare. \
We could've launched it much sooner, but what stopped us then is that we lacked the confidence to say it's enough.

It is easy to toss yourself in a dozen directions attempting to differentiate in each. \
It is harder, and scarier, to take your bets knowing that you might be wrong.

I do wonder what people will think of gitdot. \
And though I know that it will likely be tepid, I'm getting excited for it too. 

Thank you, \
—baepaul
