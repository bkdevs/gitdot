CI is something we've thought about for quite a while.
I've written about it and explored the space in some detail [prior](https://gitdot.io/weeks/8).

Much of what is written there, we still stick to as requirements.
CI will be:
1. Locally testable
2. Universally reproducible
3. Secure by design

But what was made clear after attempting to build CI is that it is very hard to build right.
We do want to ensure that what we ship here is good — and something you can rely on.

So while the implementation details and engineering will certainly change, do know that we will meet the requirements above.
